About me

I am a master's thesis student at the Georgia Institute of Technology in Aerospace Engineering, advised by Professor Yongxin Chen, working on state estimation and planning. I am supported by the NSF Graduate Research Fellowship (GRFP), and I plan to eventually pursue a PhD.

Previously, I worked on hypersonic guidance and control with Professor John Dec related to the HyperSat program. I have also spent extensive time in the startup world, having interned in guidance, navigation, and control (GNC) at both Astranis and Mach Industries.

Research Interests

  • Navigation and Mapping

    Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM), state estimation, computer vision.

  • Stochastic Control

    Safe trajectory optimization, path integral control, generative AI.
